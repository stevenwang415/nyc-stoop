# REVIEW — 2026-07-15 (personas + Apple lens; anchor: 9.6 / 9.7)

## 1. Headline

Today killed the last user-found **data-loss bug** (saving plan #2 silently
destroyed plan #1) and turned the saved plan from an afterthought into the
document people will actually read on the sidewalk. Plans are now a library:
save many, each named by its trip date, rename/delete per plan, identical
saves refuse to duplicate, legacy single snapshots migrate losslessly. The
saved-plan view gained the three things its reader needs — travel connectors
with subway detail, per-day cost/commute summaries, and the user's own card
notes — while joining the house style (serif names, day-hue pills, ≈$/person,
one card grammar for stops and meals). The hands-on pass also caught a
genuine tap-stealing bug (Leaflet map controls floating above the My Plans
overlay) before any reviewer or user could. **Maya 9.6 → 9.7, Tom & Rachel
9.7 → 9.75.**
