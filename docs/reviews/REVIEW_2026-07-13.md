## 4. Persona walkthroughs (scenarios that changed)

**Maya.** Types "attaboy" — no dead row now; empty state hands her to Google
Maps without pretending. Types "ariana" — the concert appears with venue + date
and opens the full sheet: this is the first pass where search serves her
"what's on" instinct. On the map she taps "Jazz" in the legend and pins filter;
rotates with two fingers; compass resets. Friction: with the legend expanded on
her iPhone 13 mini, the Go-to-My-Trip pill crowds the legend's right edge (S-1).

**Tom & Rachel.** Their Day 1 header now says `🚶 ~7 min · 🚇 ~41 min ·
≈$140/person` — the two questions ("how much walking? what'll it cost?") they
used to leave the app to answer. They rename the snapshot "MY first day at NYC";
it survives reload with its estimates on the card. Regression they'd notice:
Autumn Rhythm's page is now a colored tile, not the painting (S-4) — correct
legally, but the art-pilgrimage flow is visually poorer than last week.

## 6. Issues

**Must-fix (before Friday's submission)**
- **M-1 · Feedback address is the founder's personal Gmail.** Who: you, then
  users when it changes. Consequence: the address ships inside the binary;
  changing it later costs an app-store update. Fix: create the business
  address, swap `FEEDBACK_EMAIL` (one line), rebuild. Decided, not yet done.

**Should-fix (v1.0.1 unless trivial before Friday)**
- **S-1 · Map bottom-edge collision.** Expanded legend (≤160px, left) + Go-to-
  My-Trip pill (centered) can overlap on ≤375pt screens. Who: Maya. Fix:
  auto-collapse legend when the pill appears, or right-align the pill.
- **S-2 · Feedback fails silently with no configured mail app.** Who: exactly
  the users motivated enough to write. Fix now: hint line under Send; fix
  right: the backlogged `/feedback` endpoint.
- **S-3 · Compass control always visible** (`closeOnZeroBearing:false`) — chrome
  for a gesture most users never make (weight ≠ importance). Fix: let it hide
  at north (flip the flag) once the tutorial mentions rotation.
- **S-4 · Twelve heroless work pages.** Fallback tile is designed, but Pollock/
  Rothko pages lost their strongest element. Fix: "View at metmuseum.org →"
  object-page links (legal, useful, 20 min).
- **S-5 · Plan-card trip estimate sums stops only; day headers include meal
  legs.** The two totals can visibly disagree on meal-heavy days. Fix: reuse
  one sequence builder for both.

**Polish**
- P-1 · Plans-only page: title "Saved plans" + section heading "SAVED PLANS"
  double-label the same thing.
- P-2 · Rotation plugin loads from unpkg at runtime; offline first-load loses
  rotation silently (acceptable degrade — document it).
- P-3 · Tutorial emoji (📍🎨🗓️) vs. the app's line-icon language on adjacent
  chrome.
