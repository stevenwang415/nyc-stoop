Justifications, per anti-inflation rules:
- **First impression:** first-visit coach cards on all four tabs (the Map's
  pattern, generalized — with a real React reconciliation bug found and fixed
  in the process), and a Settings sheet that went from 12 rows spilling off
  screen to 7 breathing ones that swipe closed.
- **Content (visitors +0.1):** the travel connectors grew from "~20 min
  subway" into an actual routing companion: one-transfer routes ("F at 2 Av →
  change at Bleecker St to the 6"), exit-walk honesty ("then a ~10 min walk"),
  and eleven text-only bus corridors with board/alight anchors — chosen by
  structure (one-seat rides beat transfer+walk), never by fake
  minute-precision. This is the feature a paper guidebook can't do.
- **Trust (+0.1, capped):** the "YOURS" label lie is dead (seeds now badge
  STOOP everywhere; only user-created places say Yours), raw ids no longer
  leak into Saved Places, add-a-place works for every source, and stale links
  (Sushi Yasuda) got fixed. Capped because today's four real bugs were all
  USER-found, not test-found — the headless suite grew to cover them only
  after the fact.
- **Navigation:** swipe-to-dismiss now works on every sheet (the shared
  BottomSheet was missing `touch-action:none` on its scroll area — handle
  worked, body didn't; the neighborhoods sheet had no gesture at all), the
  Go-to-My-Trip pill keys on plan-or-saves, and Choose Stops reads as two
  honestly-labeled groups with an 8-chip fold instead of a 30-chip wall.
